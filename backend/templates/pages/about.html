The Elleden Hotel (K) Limited

A premier hospitality establishment located in the heart of Marsabit, Kenya.

The Elleden Hotel is designed to offer an unparalleled hospitality experience within a calm, secure, and accessible environment. Whether guests are visiting for business, leisure, conferences, or special events, The Elleden Hotel provides exceptional comfort with modern amenities that meet international standards.

Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted partner for travelers, corporate organizations, NGOs, government bodies, and local communities seeking reliable hospitality services in Marsabit.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional hospitality through quality services, personalized care, and a welcoming environment that exceeds guest expectations.

Our Vision

To be the preferred hotel in Marsabit County, recognized for excellence, comfort, and memorable guest experiences.
